Facts about Carbon

  • Carbon is one of the most important elements to life on planet Earth
  • It consists of 6 protons and 6 eletrons.
  • It forms more compounds than any other element and forms the basis to all plant and animal life
  • Carbon makes up about 18% of the human body. So if you weigh 100 pounds, your body containsaround 18 pounds of carbon.
  • In its diamond allotrope, carbon is the hardest known substance in nature.
  • Carbon also has the highest melting point of all the elements.
  • Carbon is used in some way in most every industry in the world such as fuel in the form of coal, methane gas, and crude oil.
  • It is known to form nearly 10 million different compounds.
