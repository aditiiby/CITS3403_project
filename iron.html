Facts about Iron

  • Iron its pure form iron is a fairly soft, grayish metal. It is very reactive and will readily corrode or rust.
  • It consists of 26 protons and 26 eletrons.
  • Iron is the most naturally magnetic of the elements.
  • Iron is the most abundant element in the Earth.
  • The most important use of iron is in iron alloys which include cast iron, pig iron, wrought iron, and steel.
  • Steel from iron is both inexpensive and very strong and is used in the production of all sorts of items including cars, ships, buildings, and tools.
  • In the human body iron is a major component of the blood which carries oxygen throughout the body from the lungs.
  • Iron is mentioned in the Book of Genesis in the Bible.
